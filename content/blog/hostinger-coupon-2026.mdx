## Common Mistakes People Make With Hostinger Coupons

**Using expired codes** — This is the most common issue. Always check when the code was last verified before spending time trying it.

**Applying to the wrong plan** — Some coupon codes only apply to specific plans. A code for Business Shared may not work on Single Shared, and vice versa. Read the terms of the coupon before applying.

**Choosing monthly billing** — Coupon or no coupon, monthly billing on Hostinger is significantly more expensive per month than annual billing. Always go annual or longer to get the real deal.

**Missing the renewal price** — This is important. Coupon codes apply to your **first billing period only**. When your plan renews, it renews at regular price. Set a calendar reminder before renewal and look for a new coupon code at that point.

**Not comparing with the current sale price** — Sometimes Hostinger's ongoing sale price is already lower than what a coupon would give you. Always check the current listed price before assuming a coupon is saving you money.
