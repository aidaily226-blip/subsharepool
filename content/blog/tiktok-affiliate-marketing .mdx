# TikTok Affiliate Marketing — How Creators Like Keith Lee Are Making Real Money

If you've been on TikTok at any point in the last couple years, you've probably seen Keith Lee. The guy walks into a small restaurant, orders food, eats it on camera, gives his honest take — and by the next morning that restaurant has a line going around the block.

He didn't get famous by selling things. He got famous by being honest. And that is actually the whole lesson right there.

Then there's YouTube revenue, merchandise, and restaurant partnership deals stacked on top of all that.

So yes, the number is real. But the more interesting question is how he got there.

---

## TikTok Affiliate Marketing Works Because of Trust — Not Just Followers

Keith Lee's first videos were just him being genuinely honest about food. No big production. No fancy editing. Just a guy saying what he actually thought.

That consistency over time is what made people trust him. And when people trust you, they buy what you recommend. Brands figured that out pretty quick and started paying accordingly.

This is the part most people skip when they think about affiliate marketing. They see the $150,000 per post number and think the goal is to get a million followers. But the actual goal is to get people to trust what you say. Followers without trust don't convert. Trust without a massive following still converts.

Keith Lee could have 500,000 followers and still be making serious money because his audience actually believes him.
