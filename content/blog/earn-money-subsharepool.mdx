okay so we have been building SubSharePool for a while now and the most common question we get from Indian users is — *"bhai isme kuch earning bhi hogi?"*

Fair question honestly.

So we decided to try something. We are calling it our Referral Program Beta — emphasis on beta because we are still figuring things out and nothing here is guaranteed or confirmed. ---

## What Is This Program

It is a simple refer and earn system. You share your unique referral link with people. When they use your link to sign up and engage with the platform, you earn a small amount.

We built this because we want real people using SubSharePool — not just visitors who sign up and disappear. The referral program rewards people who bring genuine users who actually do something on the platform.

---

## How Much Can You Earn

The amounts are small right now because this is a test:

```
₹1 — when someone signs up using your referral link
₹1 — when you post your first genuine listing
```

Maximum ₹2 per referral. Maximum 10 referrals per month. So the maximum you can earn in a month is ₹20.

We know ₹20 is not going to make anyone rich. That is not the point right now. The point is to test the system, see how it works, reward early supporters, and then decide whether to scale it up or change it based on what we learn.

---

## How to Get Your Referral Link

1. Go to [SubSharePool](https://subsharepool.com)
2. Sign in with Google
3. Go to your Dashboard
4. Click the **Referrals** tab
5. Your unique link is there — copy and share it anywhere

---

## How to Get Paid

When your balance reaches ₹20 you can request a withdrawal via UPI directly from your dashboard. We will manually verify and transfer within 48 hours.

We are doing manual verification for now because this is a beta program and we want to check that earnings are genuine before paying out.

---

## Important Rules — Please Read

We want this to be fair for everyone so there are some rules:

**India only** — The referral program is currently available only for users in India. We might expand later but for now it is India only.

**Genuine listings only** — The ₹1 for posting your first listing is for a real listing. A real service you actually want to share — Netflix, Spotify, ChatGPT, a carpool, anything genuine. If we see fake or spam listings we will not approve the earning.

**No self referral** — You cannot refer yourself with a different account. The system detects this automatically.

**No fake accounts** — Creating multiple accounts to game the system will result in immediate suspension of all accounts and cancellation of all earnings.

**We verify manually** — Every earning goes through manual approval before payment. If something looks suspicious we will not approve it. We would rather pay less people fairly than pay everyone including bad actors.
